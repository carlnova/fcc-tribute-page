Edward Van Halen

Musician

  • 1955 - Born in Nijmegen, Netherlands
  • 1962 - The Van Halen family moved to the UnitedStates
  • 1972 - Eddie and his brother Alex formed Mammoth
  • 1974 - Mammoth is renamed Van Halen
  • 1977 - Van Halen signs first record deal with Warner
  • 1979 - Voted Guitar Player's Best Rock Guitarist
  • 1980 - Marries actress Valerie Bertinelli
  • 1991 - Valerie Bertinelli gives birth to Wolfgang William Van Halen
  • 1992 - Band wins Grammy for best Hard Rock performance
  • 2005 - Eddie and wife, Valerie Bertinelli, filed for divorce
  • 2007 - Van Halen is inducted into Rock and Roll Hall of fame
  • 2009 - Marries Janie Liszewski
  • 2020 - Passes away, after a long battle with cancer

"Music kept me off the streets and out of trouble and gave me something that was mine that no one could take away from me."

-- Edward Van Halen
